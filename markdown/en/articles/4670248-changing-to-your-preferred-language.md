# Changing to your preferred language

You will be able to change your preferred language on the [preferences page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/6880968-account-page#h_87963579a7) of your [account](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/6880968-account-page).

[YouTube video](https://www.youtube.com/embed/fwQtOwQsTN8?rel=0)

## 1. Finding the language setting

Profile->Account->Preferences You can always find this page by clicking on your profile picture on the top right and clicking on account. Then, in the left navigation menu, you will find the [preference page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/6880968-account-page#h_87963579a7) where you can find the language setting.

## 2. Available languages

At the moment Catenda Hub is available in 21 languages: English, Danish, German, Spanish, Català, French, Indonesian, Japanese, Norwegian bokmål, Norwegian Nynorsk, Dutch, Polish, Thai, Portuguese (Brazilian), Swedish, Hungarian, Italian, Latvian, Vietnamese, and simplified and traditional Chinese.
