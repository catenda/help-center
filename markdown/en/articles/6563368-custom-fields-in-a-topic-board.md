# Custom fields in a topic board

After configuring the settings of your custom fields, you can now add these fields to your topic board

You are in the "**topics"** section

1\. Choose the settings of your topic board

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/01-intro.png)

2\. click on **add a custom field**

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/02-intro.png)

**3**. Select the field to be added

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/03-intro.png)

4\. You can mark this field as required, ( Yes/No)

5\. Set a default value,

6\. Validate to save.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/04-intro.png)

When finished, you can preview your custom fields.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/05-intro.png)

You can now enter the fields on your next topics !

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/06-intro.png)

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/tv2vef51/07-intro.gif)
