# Statistics page

The statistics page can be found as a sub page to the [topics page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670271-issues-page).

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/2s7g3kx3/01-intro.png)

Here is an example of what a statistics page can look like

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/2s7g3kx3/02-intro.png)

## 1. Overview

You will have an overview of all the open and closed topics on the board.

## 2. Distribution

**Accumulated status**

Here you have the accumulated status on open and closed topics.

**Type distribution**

The type distribution on the different topics.

## 3. Plan reliability

Plan reliability measures your ability to close topics before it's due date.

Here you will see milestones and due dates over time.
