# Freemium

While enterprise customers have the option to create their own Catenda Hub projects, regular users will be able to create projects on the projects page with the freemium plan. This will create a project with limited functionality where some features are locked. A freemium project can look something like this.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/04se8rbt/01-intro.png)

## 1. **Creating a freemium project**

Freemium projects can be made in the following ways:

### 1.1 **New account**

If you create an account through the sign up page you will automatically be guided to make a freemium project.

### 1.2 **Existing account**

If you have an existing account and are not the owner of an organization you will be able to make 1 freemium project per account by clicking on the [new project button](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/8400797-projects-page#h_085de048f4) on the top right of the projects page.

## 2. **Upgrading a freemium project**

Freemium projects can easily be upgraded to a full access project. If you would like to upgrade your project, please contact support with the black chat button next to your profile picture on the top right or via [support@catenda.com](mailto:support@catenda.com)

### 2.1 **Existing customer**

Existing customers will often have their own environment where to freemium projects can be transferred if needed.

### 2.2 **New customer**

For new customers an environment can be made so they can continue from their freemium project.

### 2.3 **Student**

If you can prove that you are a student and would like to do try Catenda Hub we are happy to open up a project for you during the desired period. You will need the following:

1\. Please contact us from your school email address 2\. Proof of enrolment or student id card 3\. Requested timeframe.

### 2.4 **Trial**

If you would like to test out the full functionality of Catenda Hub we are more than happy to give you full access for an initial 14 days. Please contact us for this as mentioned above. Towards the end of your trial period you will be contacted by one of our sales associates to discuss further options. There is always room to expand this trial period should this be desired.

## 3. **Content panel**

The content panel is the only panel that is available to freemium users. You can see it as activated on the top right:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/04se8rbt/02-content-panel.png)

### 3.1 **Dashboard**

**Latest notifications** Here you will see the latest notifications that have been generated in this project.

**Members** Here you will see all the members that are part of the project.

Locked Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670286-models-page) to read more about the models page.

**Collections** Locked Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/6344318-collections-page) to read more about the collections page.
