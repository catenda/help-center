# Dashboard - Catenda Site

After selecting your project on the [projects page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7733978-projects-page-catenda-site) you will be taken to your project's topics dashboard,

On this page you can view the status of your topics, choose or change which issue boards you want displayed and filter topics.

1. To change issue boards, select an issue board from the issue board dropdown list
1. Choose the filter you want to use to see your topics
    - Open topics (the selected issue board)
    - All open topics (all issue boards)
    - Closed topics (the selected issue board)
    - All closed topics  (all issue boards)
    - Requested by me
    - All candidate topics
    - Overdue topics
    - Due within 2 weeks
