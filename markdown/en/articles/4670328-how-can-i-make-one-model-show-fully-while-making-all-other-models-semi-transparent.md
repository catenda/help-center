# How can I make one model show fully, while making all other models semi-transparent?

1. Open all models in the 3D-viewer
1. Open the tree-panel (shortcut is shift-3).
1. Select the model you want to look at (“isolate”), and click the top node of that list. This makes all elements in this model selected.
1. Right click on the top-node and select “Isolate”. All objects in this model will now show fully, while all objects from other models will be semi-transparent.
