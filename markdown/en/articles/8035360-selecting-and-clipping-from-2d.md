# Selecting and Clipping from 2D

### **Clipping in the model from 2D**

Start by aligning your 2D plan the way you want by using the [Rotate tool](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4854537-2d-viewer#h_c70412a63f). Next choose the [Section: Create clipping planes](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4854537-2d-viewer#h_2265eef562) and drag a rectangle to indicate the vertical clipping planes. Select the height of the current storey/floor or the full height. Click Clip region.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/xyh08ptx/01-clipping-in-the-model-from-2d.png)

You can now [move the clipping planes](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670253-clipping-planes#moving-the-clipping-plane) as normal by selecting the relevant plane in the clipping widget (bottom left), or by pressing "Shift" and clicking on the plane in the viewer. Planes can be [removed](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670253-clipping-planes#remove-clipping-planes) individually by selecting a plane and pressing "Del" or all at once by right clicking anywhere in the 3D viewer and selecting "Remove clipping planes"

### **Selecting objects from 2D**

[Section: Select objects](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4854537-2d-viewer#h_9ba868389c) will work in a similar way and will select anything touched by the "virtual box" defined by the selection rectangle and height. To get back to normal selection in 2D click [Select](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4854537-2d-viewer#h_6e9b382c42).
