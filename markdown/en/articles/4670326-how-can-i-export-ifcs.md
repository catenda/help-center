# How can I export IFCs?

You can export an IFC file by going to **Models** (1) and then choosing the **Exports** (2) button.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/3gv5z5st/01-intro.png)

On the next page, you can see the history of previous downloads and redownload them individually.

If you want to merge several models into one file, a "New export" must be created.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/3gv5z5st/02-intro.png)

The next step is to choose the export options. Choose whether to archive the the file as a .zip or .xls file Then choose which models and which revisions of those models will be included in the export.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/3gv5z5st/03-intro.png)

To export, scroll down the page and find point number 6.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/3gv5z5st/04-intro.png)
