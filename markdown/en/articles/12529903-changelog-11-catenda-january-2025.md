# Changelog 11 Catenda - January, 2025

Hello everyone,

thank you all for being so active on our platform in these winter months. We have enjoyed an amazing winter period and can prodly say that there has been more stability on the platform around this time than previous years. At the same time we have continued to listen to your request about the approval workflow overhaul that was brand new as of the last changelog. While some administrators have learned how to configure workflows and others been busy at work approving on behalf of their team there are a few checkboxes for us left to complete to tie everything together. A focus going forward will be creating a way to lock down an audit trail of the different actions that have occured in the approval process so documentation can be provided as proof that approval checks are being performed by the different teams. With the new members page it is easier than ever to configure team membership so let your administrators know if you feel like you are not in the right team or a team is missing in the project! ❄️⛄☃️🥶🧊🎿⛸️🏒⛷️🏂🏔️🌨️

## 2. **Bugs Resolved**

Thanks to your valuable feedback we have been able to squash many topics that you may or may not have noticed existed. Below is a list of smaller changes that have been made by the development team as a result of conversations with users.

### 2.1 **Topics**

- It is once again possibe to click on the play button of a markup snapshot to play the annotations on the document instead of getting a completely white screen.
- Text search improvements on the topics page.

### 2.2 **Documents**

- It is once again possible to open folders after a white screen was displayed when opening a folder in a 15 minute period.

### 2.3 **Models**

- The amount of memory dedicated to generating excel exports of filtered rows in the objects table has been increased so the excel export properly gets generated and an infinite spinner is no longer displayed.
- It is now much faster and the page is less likely to freeze when selecting 50000+ elements in the objeects table.
- When grids are enabled and the model is transformed in the Catenda Hub 3D viewer (browser only) The grids always followed, now, when you refresh the page and load the transformed model at its transformed location the grids are correctly displayed at that transformed location as well.

### 2.4 **Plugins and integrations**

- It is once again possible for users that have MFA enabled to open the content of projects via plugins and integrations.
- Catenda as an application
    - It is once again possible to have both 3D viewer and the content panels open at the same time instead of one at a time on 11 Inch iPads

## 3. **User voices completed**

### 3.1 **Topics**

- It is once again possible to search on partial words in topic titles, descriptions in comments. The topic number search has now been separated from this search allowing more granularity when searching for topics specificlly by their topic number on Catenda.

### 3.2 **Documents**

- Notifications are now being sent out to users that are part of teams that belong in three different categories.
As part of an approval submitter team, as part of a team that is in one of the workflow steps and as part of the final submitter team.
There are notifications about new information like information being submitted, and edited.
There are notificatiotions about responsibility like when it is time for a reviewer team to do their review or when it is time for the final reviewer to publish the documents in the approval request.
And there are notifications about actions being performed like all reviews in a step being submitted by the different teams in the step or the final closing of an approval request by the final reviewer team.
- On the final step of an approval the instead of just approving and rejecting it is now possible for someone from the final approver team to approve with comment. This is typically used in the situation where the final approver would like to approve on behalf of the approver team but has some reservations about the document. Maybe a small change needs to be made, maybe there is an acceptable flaw in the document.

### 3.3 **Settings**

- It is now possible to configure team membership by selecting on or more members in the list and making changes to update them in the right menu instead of having to go to the team and having to add member by member.
- Date field - A new custom field has been made available of type Date.
If this field is made available for topics or documents each date field that has been made available for a topic/document can be given a date by clicking on it and selecting a date in a calendar. 
Date fields are not available for naming conventions but they are available via the API.
