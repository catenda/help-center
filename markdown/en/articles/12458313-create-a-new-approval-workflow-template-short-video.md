# Create a new approval workflow template - Short video

[Guidde video](https://embed.app.guidde.com/playbooks/eu_fUBwAB5gUE7vhCJsnU1kGi)

In this tutorial, you will learn how to create a new approval workflow template in Catenda Hub.

### **1. Introduction**

Before you start, ensure you have the necessary permissions to configure workflows and know the teams involved in the approval process.

![Introduction](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/01-1-introduction.png)

### **2. Go to the Documents section**

Go to the documents section to open the main document management area, where you can access various settings and workflows.

![Go to the Documents section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/02-2-go-to-the-documents-section.png)

### **3. Access the Settings section**

Acces the Settings section to open the configuration options for your documents and workflows.

![Access the Settings section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/03-3-access-the-settings-section.png)

### **4. Go to the Approvals section**

Go to the Approvals section to access the approval settings and workflow templates.

![Go to the Approvals section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/04-4-go-to-the-approvals-section.png)

### **5. Click here to configure workflows**

Click here to configure workflows to manage and create approval workflow templates.

![Click here to configure workflows](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/05-5-click-here-to-configure-workflows.png)

### **6. Click here**

Press the plus button to start creating a new approval workflow template.

![Click here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/06-6-click-here.png)

### **7. Specify the name of your new workflow template**

Specify the name of your new workflow template to clearly identify this workflow for future use.

![Specify the name of your new workflow template](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/07-7-specify-the-name-of-your-new-workflow-template.png)

### **8. Set the start time**

Set the start time for when the approval process should begin.

![Set the start time](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/08-8-set-the-start-time.png)

### **9. Click "09"**

Click "09" to select 9 AM as the start time for the workflow.

![Click '09'](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/09-9-click-09.png)

### **10. Click here**

Click the field to add submitters who will initiate the approval process.

![Click here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/10-10-click-here.png)

### **11. Select @Construction Workshop**

Select the @Construction Workshop team as submitters for the workflow.

![Select @Construction Workshop](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/11-11-select-construction-workshop.png)

### **12. Click here**

Click here to assign teams responsible for reviewing the submissions.

![Click here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/12-12-click-here.png)

### **13. Start typing to find your team**

Start typing to filter and find the contractor team.

![Start typing to find your team](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/13-13-start-typing-to-find-your-team.png)

### **14. Select @Contractor**

Select the @Contractor team as reviewers in the workflow.

![Select @Contractor](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/14-14-select-contractor.png)

### **15. Set the review period for the contractor team**

Set the review period for the contractor team.

![Set the review period for the contractor team](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/15-15-set-the-review-period-for-the-contractor-team.png)

### **16. Fill "3"**

Fill "3" to allow three working days for the contractor team to complete their review.

![Fill '3'](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/16-16-fill-3.png)

### **17. Activate the auto-approve feature**

Check this box to enable auto-approve to prevent bottlenecks. Documents will automatically receive approved reviews on behalf of teams that haven't responded by the due date.

![Activate the auto-approve feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/17-17-activate-the-auto-approve-feature.png)

### **18. Add a step**

Add a step to include another review stage in the workflow.

![Add a step](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/18-18-add-a-step.png)

### **19. Click here**

Click here to assign the next team responsible for review.

![Click here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/19-19-click-here.png)

### **20. Start typing to filter and find the ARC team.**

Start typing to filter and find the ARC team.

![Start typing to filter and find the ARC team.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/20-20-start-typing-to-filter-and-find-the-arc-team.png)

### **21. Select @ARC**

Select the @ARC team as reviewers in this step.

![Select @ARC](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/21-21-select-arc.png)

### **22. Set the review period for the architecture team.**

Set the review period for the architecture team.

![Set the review period for the architecture team.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/22-22-set-the-review-period-for-the-architecture-team.png)

### **23. Fill "2"**

Fill "2" to allow two working days for the ARC team to complete their review.

![Fill '2'](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/23-23-fill-2.png)

### **24. Activate the auto-approve feature**

Check this box to enable auto-approve to prevent bottlenecks.

![Activate the auto-approve feature](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/24-24-activate-the-auto-approve-feature.png)

### **25. Add a workflow step**

Add a step by repeating the same process as described previously to include another review stage in the workflow. You can add as many steps as needed for your workflow.

![Add a workflow step](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/25-25-add-a-workflow-step.png)

### **26. Assign the final approval team**

Click here to assign the final approval team.

![Assign the final approval team](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/26-26-assign-the-final-approval-team.png)

### **27. Start typing to filter and find the client team.**

Start typing to filter and find the client team.

![Start typing to filter and find the client team.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/27-27-start-typing-to-filter-and-find-the-client-team.png)

### **28. Click @Client**

Click @Client to add the client team as the final approvers.

![Click @Client](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/28-28-click-client.png)

### **29. Set the review period for the client team.**

Set the review period for the client team.

![Set the review period for the client team.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/29-29-set-the-review-period-for-the-client-team.png)

### **30. Fill "3"**

Fill "3" to allow three working days for the client team to approve the submission.

![Fill '3'](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/30-30-fill-3.png)

### **31. Define the document status after approval.**

Click here to define the document status after approval.

![Define the document status after approval.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/31-31-define-the-document-status-after-approval.png)

### **32. Select the Published status**

Click on Published to set the document status to published once approved.

![Select the Published status](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/32-32-select-the-published-status.png)

### **33. Define the document status after rejection.**

Click here to define the document status after rejection.

![Define the document status after rejection.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/33-33-define-the-document-status-after-rejection.png)

### **34. Select the Shared status**

Click on Shared to set the document status to shared if rejected.

![Select the Shared status](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/34-34-select-the-shared-status.png)

### **35. Click on Submit**

Click on the Submit button to save and activate the new approval workflow template.

![Click on Submit](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/35-35-click-on-submit.png)

### **36. Find your New approval workflow template on your workflow's list**

You can now find your new approval workflow template on your workflow's list

![Find your New approval workflow template on your workflow's list](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/81w93hf4/36-36-find-your-new-approval-workflow-template-on-your-workflow-s-list.png)

You have successfully created a new approval workflow template with multiple review steps and assigned teams. Verify the workflow by reviewing the template name, assigned teams, review periods, and statuses. Next, you can apply this workflow to your projects or customise additional templates as needed.
