# Organization configuration

Organizations are created and managed by a Catenda contact person. Contact Catenda support to make changes to the organization configuration. Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/8224886-organization-options) to read more about the different organization options that exist.

The following can be configured by a Catenda contact person:

### **Adding/removing organization owners**

It is recommended that this organization owners are kept few as projects do not have to be moved often.

### **MFA enforcement**

MFA can be enforced for all projects of an organization. MFA is recommended to be enabled on all organizations of a company as a set of projects is only as secure as its weakest link.

### **Single Sign On**

SSO is only available for enterprise customers. With SSO members with the email domain of the organization that holds the license are able to sign in with their company credentials rather than the Catenda sign in page. MFA is handeld by the company themselves for these members while externals are still subject to any MFA enforcement that is enabled.

### **Domain registration**

Add members from a domain to an organization

### **Project plan**

Total projects per organization Project expiry date

### **Document download title**

The title that downloaded documents in this organization have.

### **Public sharing**

Disable public sharing the the organization projects.
