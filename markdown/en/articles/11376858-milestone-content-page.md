# Milestone content page

The page of a milestone can be opened by clicking on a milestone on the [milestones page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670266-milestones-page). This is what the page of a milestone can look like:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/yx2925b3/01-intro.png)

## 1. **Actions**

You will be able to find the following actions towards the top right of the page of a milestone:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/yx2925b3/02-actions.png)

### 1.1 **New milestone**

Click on the green + or on the New milestone action in the action menu to create a new milestone.

### 1.2 **Archive**

If you milestone is active you will be able to archive it with this action. _Access required:_ Administrator or Milestone creator Archived milestones can be found and restored with the archived milestones actions on the [milestones page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670266-milestones-page).

## 2. **Restore**

Click on the blue restore button towards the top right to restore this milestone. _Access required:_ Administrator or Milestone creator

## 3. **Name**

The name of the milestone. Click on the pencil to edit the name. _Access required:_ Administrator or Milestone creator

## 4. **Description**

The description of the milestone. Click on the pencil to edit the description. _Access required:_ Administrator or Milestone creator

## 5. **Due date**

The due date of the milestone. Click on the date dropdown to edit the due date. _Access required:_ Administrator or Milestone creator

## 6. **Created by**

Here you will see the name of the user that created the milestone and the date the milestone was created.

## 7. **Topic statistics**

For each of the topic board where there are topics with this milestone assigned you will see a statistic that shows you what the progress of the milestone in that board is.

**Percentage** Here you will see the percentage of topics that have this milestone assigned and are closed in this topic board. Candidate topics do not count towards this percentage.

**Open/closed/candidate** Here you will see the amount topics with open, closed or candidate meta-statuses that this milestone has been assigned to. Click on the word open, closed or canidate to see the related topics in the all topics board.

**Member profile picture** Here you will first see the creator of the milestone and the unassigned picture. For each profile picture you will see an amount of topics. For the milestone creator and unassigned this amount can be 0 and will then be green. Other users will only be visible if they have any open topics to which this milestone has been assigned with an amount of open topics to which they have been assigned in red.
