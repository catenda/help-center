# Approving Documents for a New Physical Infrastructure Project

Amanda is a Municipal Engineer responsible for a new bridge construction project. Her primary role is to ensure all plans and designs meet city regulations and are safe for public use. The new approval workflow in Catenda Hub helps her manage this process efficiently and avoid costly mistakes.

### **The Use Case: Reviewing Bridge Foundation Plans**

Amanda has received a new approval request from the external **Structural Engineer** team. They have submitted the foundation plans for review. Amanda needs to examine these plans carefully, add her comments, and make a decision on whether they can move forward.

### **Reviewing and Commenting on Documents**

1. **Access your approval requests:** Amanda logs into Catenda Hub and goes to the **Approvals page**. She filters for her open approvals to see the requests assigned to her.
1. **Open the document for review:** She clicks on the request and then the "Review" button to open the foundation plans.
1. **Add markups and annotations:** As she reviews the plans, Amanda notices a detail that needs to be clarified with the **Geotechnical Consultant**. She clicks on the "Annotate" tab and uses the drawing tools to highlight the specific area in question. She saves her markup, giving it a clear name like "Foundation Markup - Geotechnical Review".
1. **Make a decision:** After reviewing the document and adding her comments, Amanda decides to "Approve" it. The **MEP Engineer** team also participates in the review and provides their feedback.
1. **Submit your decision:** She clicks "Submit" to finalize her decision. Her approval is now visible to the other teams in this review step. The **Structural Engineer** will see her comments and can respond to them.

### **Using Topics to Track and Communicate**

To make a change request about the foundation plan, Amanda creates a new topic. She names it "Request for Foundation Plan Revision" and links it directly to the document. This allows the Structural Engineer to see the new request immediately. When a new revision of the document is uploaded, Amanda will receive a notification because she is following the topic. She can also export a PDF report of all topics related to the project to share with a client or for archiving purposes.

### **What Happens After the Review**

Once all reviewer teams have submitted their decisions, the document moves to the final approval stage. This ensures that the plans are thoroughly reviewed by all relevant parties before they are formally approved for construction. The document will eventually be moved to the **Published** state, and construction workers can rely on it as a final, approved document.
