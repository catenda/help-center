# Create a new approval request

[Click here to watch](https://eu.guidde.com/share/playbooks/pVvhvQyjEqKxAf7HrqeSVv)

### 

![Quick guidde](https://static.intercomassets.com/assets/article_broken_image_placeholder.png)

### 

This tutorial guides you through creating a new approval request in Catenda Hub. If you haven't already, please watch the "Create a new approval workflow template" video before watching this one.

### **Go to [hub.catenda.com](https://hub.catenda.com/)**

### **1. Introduction**

You will learn how to select workflows, submit requests for review, add annotations, and manage approval decisions. Before starting, ensure you have access to the relevant project documents and the necessary permissions to create approval requests.

![Introduction](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-23d98aecf475.png)

### **2. Open the document for your approval request**

Open the document for your approval request. In this example, we select only one document, but you can attach multiple documents to your approval request

![Open the document for your approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-b2d4a2dab616.png)

### **3. Go to the Approvals section**

Go to the Approvals section.

![Go to the Approvals section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-086c73d1d3f4.png)

### **4. Click here to start a new approval request.**

Click here to start a new approval request.

![Click here to start a new approval request.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-df967b5a0083.png)

### **5. Choose the workflow to use for this request.**

In this section, you can choose the workflow to use for your new request.

![Choose the workflow to use for this request.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-40c2cf01c20c.png)

### **6. Select the workflow template from the list**

Select the workflow template from the dropdown menu.

![Select the workflow template from the list](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-1accb5ffd38e.png)

### **7. Give a title to your new approval request.**

Give a title to your new approval request.

![Give a title to your new approval request.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-4d0dc3947f18.png)

### **8. Click here to send your request to the teams for review.**

Click here to send your request to the teams for review.

![Click here to send your request to the teams for review.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-c4fb9d893bea.png)

### **9. Click on the Review button**

In the first step, as part of the Contractor team, you are able to review the document by clicking on the review button.

![Click on the Review button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-a379f05179dd.png)

### **10. Click on the Approve button**

After reviewing the document, click on the Approve button to accept the approval request after reviewing its details.

![Click on the Approve button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-c04331c22634.png)

### **11. Submit your approval decision**

Finally, submit your approval decision and update the request status.

![Submit your approval decision](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-8dd4098712d7.png)

### **12. Go back to the approval request**

Go back to the approval request.

![Go back to the approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-b3ad90a12665.png)

### **13. Click on the Review button**

In the second step, as part of the ARC team, you are able to review the document by clicking on the review button.

![Click on the Review button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-37efeee6715c.png)

### **14. Start annotating the document by clicking on the Annotate tab.**

This time, we want to annotate the document. Start annotating the document by clicking on the Annotate tab.

![Start annotating the document by clicking on the Annotate tab.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-cb3e20835892.png)

### **15. Give a name to your new Markup**

After annotating the document with the drawing tool of your choice, the annotation is prestored as a Markup. Give it a name in this section.

![Give a name to your new Markup](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-3aa57e59fd06.png)

### **16. Save your new Markup**

Save your new Markup to store your annotation and associate it with the document.

![Save your new Markup](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-47e68685f897.png)

### **17. Go back to the Review section**

Go back to the Review section to return to the review interface after saving your markup.

![Go back to the Review section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-748c1773a6d0.png)

### **18. Click on the Approve button**

After reviewing the document, click on the Approve button to confirm your approval of the document with the added annotations.

![Click on the Approve button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-25aefc377a83.png)

### **19. Submit your approval decision**

Finally, submit your approval decision and update the request status.

![Submit your approval decision](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-0cd89ef51a7f.png)

### **20. Go back to the approval request**

Go back to the approval request

![Go back to the approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-fc76b012d5fd.png)

### **21. Click on the Review button**

In the third step, as part of the MEP team, you are able to review the document by clicking on the review button.

![Click on the Review button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-5e56a58c30f1.png)

### **22. Click on the Reject button**

This time, the MEP team rejects the approval request based on their assessment.

![Click on the Reject button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-8cd3acefe6fc.png)

### **23. Submit your approval decision**

Finally, submit your approval decision and update the request status.

![Submit your approval decision](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-297a2000fe8f.png)

### **24. Go back to the approval request**

Go back to the approval request.

![Go back to the approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-e63e4a284b59.png)

### **25. Click on the Review button**

In the final step, as part of the Client team, you are able to finalizethe document by clicking on the review button.

![Click on the Review button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-ceaac7b43c8f.png)

### **26. Approve the request**

As the final approval team, you are able to reconsider and approve the request if appropriate, even if a team has rejected it in a previous step.

![Approve the request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-aa5c05424d26.png)

### **27. Access the Markup section**

Before final submission, you can always access the Markup section to view all annotations associated with the approval request.

![Access the Markup section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-5c1081857eb3.png)

### **28. View the Markup on the document**

Click on the play button of one of the Markups to display the Markup on the document.

![View the Markup on the document](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-6a5d83ef130d.png)

### **29. Return to the Review section**

Return to the Review section after inspecting markups.

![Return to the Review section](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-b5969fd08417.png)

### **30. Submit your approval decision**

Finally, submit your approval decision and finalize the request status.

![Submit your approval decision](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-bd466a0906c1.png)

### **31. Go back to the approval request**

Go back to the approval request.

![Go back to the approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-ce2fcbdffc13.png)

### **32. Overview of the approval request**

Here, you can get an overview of the approval request, and notice that publishing has succeeded. The approval request is now closed.

![Overview of the approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-8db21736cb5e.png)

### **33. Click on the Documents tab**

Click on the Documents tab to get on overview on the documents attached to the approval request.

![Click on the Documents tab](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-2eb58e828d8a.png)

### **34. Overview of the documents attached to the approval request**

Here, you get an overview on the documents attached to the approval request. You can see the document approval status at each step, and its final transition from shared to published.

![Overview of the documents attached to the approval request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/x4sc7qmc/inline-3a8f25c3f41a.png)

You have successfully created and managed a new approval request, including submitting it for review, adding annotations, approving, rejecting, and finalising the process. To verify success, ensure the approval request status reflects your final decision and all comments are saved.
