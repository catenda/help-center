# Resizing of columns

You will know you resize a column if you see a light gray box hanging from the line between two headers. When you hover your mouse over the line between two columns headers you can drag to change the width of a column.

**Cursor change:** You will know you are hovering over the right place when your cursor changes to a resize pointer

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/dysbq1me/01-intro.png)

**Double click:** If you double click on the drag spot the column will automatically expand to the size of its contents.
