# Can a standard member invite new members to a project?

Yes, but the administrator needs to give the member a special right to do so. Click on the settings menu

![mceclip0.png](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/8jh8rzpm/01-intro.png)

on the sidebar and select the "Invite new members" item under the "Access control" panel.

![mceclip1.png](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/8jh8rzpm/02-intro.png)

Open the panel and click "Edit access". Next select the member(s) or team(s) that shall be given the right to invite others and click "Save.
