### 1.13 Manage, sort and filter topics in your list.

You now have the ability to filter and sort the different topics in your list. They can be sorted using the fully configurable table: by type, status, label, due date, last topic created, assigned person, etc. You can also filter topics from the filter panel that can be activated on the left. Here you can filter topics in the same way as for sorting topics, and thus isolate for example topics with a specific status.

![Manage, sort and filter topics in your list.](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/mmrex0mx/12-12-manage-sort-and-filter-topics-in-your-list.png)
