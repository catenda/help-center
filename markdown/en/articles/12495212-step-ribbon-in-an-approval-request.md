# Step ribbon in an approval request

The step ribbon can be found towards the top of an approval request page of a [draft](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/12495175-draft-approval-request-page), [open or closed](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/12494960-open-or-closed-approval-request-page) approval request for projects that enabled shared revisions after 2 October 2025. In the step ribbon a preview of all the approval review steps in an approval are displayed. This is what the approval request step ribbon can look like for an approval request where an approval workflow is selected with the maximum amount of approval request steps:

![Approval step ribbon. Step 2 Due date Final approval 10 steps](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/01-intro.png)

Projects where shared revisions were enabled before 2 October 2025 will see the legacy approval request page instead.

## 1. **Created approval request step**

Hover over the created step to see information about the creation of the approval request. See the name of te member that created the approval request. At the time of creation the member has to have been part of a submitter team configured for this approval workflow. See when the approval request was created.

![Approval ribben request step hover created by username due date](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/02-created-approval-request-step.png)

### 1.1 **Created step in draft approval request**

Thisis what the created step can look like in a draft approval request:

![Approval ribbon created step red overdue date final approval check](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/03-created-step-in-draft-approval-request.png)

Draft approvals pass their creation date ass soon as they get created. The creation date therefore always looks red.

## 2. **Passed approval request step**

Hover over a passed approval step to see information about the passed approval step. See the due date for the passed approval step. When the due date has passed it will be red. See which teams are configured for this approval step.

![Overdue request step approval ribbon hover due date in red teams to review mentioned](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/04-passed-approval-request-step.png)

## 3. **Current approval request step**

When an approval request setep is the current step the due date for the current approval step can be seen in the approval request step preview. When the due date has passed it will be red. Hover the current approval request step to see information about the current approval request step.

![Approval ribbon review step hover with 10 reviewer teams all listed in popup](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/05-current-approval-request-step.png)

Auto-advance enabled - The approval request will automatically move to the next step after the due date Auto-advance disabled - The approval request remains in the step after it has passed its due date and will only advance after all reviewers have submitted their validation review. See which reviewer teams are configured for this approval request step.

## 4. **Future approval request step**

Hover over a future approval step to see information about the future approval step.

![Approval request step ribbon review step hover with one team to review not due yet](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/06-future-approval-request-step.png)

See which reviewer teams are configured for this approval request step. Future steps do not have a due date yet as the amount of working days to complete the step will start as sonn as the previous step has ended.

## 5. Final approval step

When the final approval step is the current step the due date for the final approval step can be seen in the step preview. When the due date has passed it will be red. Future final approval steps do not have a due date yet as the amount of working days to complete the step will start as sonn as the previous step has ended. Hover the current approval request step to see information about the current approval request step.

![Approval request final approval step hover with final review team listed in popup](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/07-final-approval-step.png)

See which approver team is configured for the final approval step. A member in the final approver team can make the final validation decision about approving or rejecting the documents in this approval request based on the validation decisions made by each of the teams in each of the steps. When the validating member has submitted their validation as approved or rejected for each of the documents the approval workflow will be closed. A final approval has to always be submitted to close an approval request. There is no auto-advance for the final approval step.

When all final validations are submitted the approval request will be closed.

![Approval request step ribbon closed approval final step closed in green with date of closing](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/1bshvqk0/08-final-approval-step.png)

The final approval request step then gets a green background. The date of when all final validations are submitted is displayed.
