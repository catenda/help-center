# Projects page - Catenda Site

After [downloading, installing and having logged in](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/6530821-download-your-mobile-app-catenda-site) to Catenda Site you will be presented with the projects page. You can find the projects page by tapping on the name of your project in the [menu](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7338516-navigation-menu-catenda-site).

![Choose a project](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/2g7dc990/01-intro.png)

You can filter your projects by tapping on the drop-down list **(A)**:

1. Alphabetical order
1. Last updated projects
1. Favorites
1. Offline projects

or by searching in the search bar **(B)**:

![Name, Last updated, Favorites, Offline](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/2g7dc990/02-intro.png)
