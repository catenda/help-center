# Edit a topic comment

> How to edit a comment in a topic

You can edit the comment(s) of a topic you have already commented on:

1. Click on the hamburger menu (3 horizontal dots) of the comment concerned,
1. Click on : Click on "**Edit"**,

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/pwjqg6c5/01-intro.png)

3\. Edit your comment, 4\. "**Save**" your changes.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/pwjqg6c5/02-intro.png)

5\. You can also see the history of changes by clicking on the hamburger menu and then on : "**View changes**"

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/pwjqg6c5/03-intro.png)
