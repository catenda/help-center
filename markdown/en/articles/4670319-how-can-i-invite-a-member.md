# How can I invite a member?

You will be able to invite new members to the project on the [invite members page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670263-invite-members-page).

_Access required:_ Access to [inviting new members](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670273-project-settings-page#h_f82a1850f3) on the project settings page.

Follow the steps below to invite a new member.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/203nmrp5/01-intro.png)

1\. Fill in email

2\. Press enter

3\. Optional - Choose the role/access

_Access required:_ Project administrator

4\. Optional - Choose the teams the member should be apart of.

_Access required:_ Project administrator

5\. Press the Add button

6\. Optional - Choose language

7\. Optional - Write a message

8\. Send invite

Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670263-invite-members-page) to read more about the invite members page.
