# Catenda QR-Code scanning

Scanning the QR-code with your smartphone will take you to a verification page where the following results will appear depending on the version of the PDF;

1. Your revision is the latest version of the PDF: **Document valid**
1. Your revision is an older version of the PDF: **Document invalid**
1. You scanned a placeholder: **You scanned a placeholder, this document is not published**
