# Navigation menu - Catenda Site

It is possible to close this menu by tapping on the dark area outside the menu. You can also close the menu by dragging down from its top or anywhere else inside of the menu

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/0q054pwc/01-intro.png)

1. _[Projects page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7733978-projects-page-catenda-site)_ - Here you can switch between projects
1. _[Dashboard](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7213817-dashboard-catenda-site)_ - Project dashboard.
1. _[Issues](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7734073-issues-page-catenda-site)_ - the last opened issue board
1. _[2D / 3D](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7734138-2d-3d-catenda-site)_ -  The [2D viewer](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7748370-2d-viewer-catenda-site) and the [3D viewer](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7744403-3d-viewer-catenda-site)
1. [Documents](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7774637-documents-catenda-site) - The documents section
1. _[Settings](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7733833-settings-catenda-site)_ - The Settings page
