# List View in a topic board

The list view in the topics table on the [topics page ](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670271-topics-page)topic board is enabled by default. If table view has been enabled, list view can be enabled again [towards the top](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670271-topics-page#h_6a5895256b) of the topic board. The list view in the topics table of the topics page can often be good to use when more than one person is following along with what is happening on the screen. While less topics than in the table view are displayed at once the information is spaced out more which makes the view easy to understand at a glance, even for someone who has never seen a topic board before. Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/11748020-tables-on-catenda) to read general information about lists on Catenda.

## 1. **Sort dropdown**

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/a6yf292m/01-sort-dropdown.png)

In the list view of a topic board it is possible to sort topics by:

1. Newest
1. Oldest
1. Recently updated
1. Least recently updated
1. Due date, newest first
1. Due date, oldest first
1. Custom field, descending
1. Custom field, ascending

## 2. **Topics list**

### 2.1 **Row content**

**Topic row** Opening the content of a topic row opens the topic page for that topic. Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/8400566-topic-header-the-subject-of-a-topic) to read more about the topic page.

### 2.2 **Cards**

Each row in the topics list contains a topic card with visual ques about the contents of each topic. Only fields that have been configured in the topic will be displayed in its card. This is what a topic with full activity can look like:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/a6yf292m/02-cards.png)

Title Topic title

**Topic board** The topic board that the topic is in. Within a topic board these are all the same, but when looking at topics in the all topics topic board there may topics from different boards.

**Statuses** Topic status is displayed in a box with a colored circle.

**Labels** Topic [labels](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670265-creating-a-new-label) (tags) are displayed in boxes with a tag icon.

**Assigned to** Topic assignee.

**Requested by** Topic requestee.

**Due** The due date of the topic. The calendar icon and text of the text are: Gray - If the date if further than two weeks ahead in time. Orange - If the date if less than two weeks ahead in time. Red - If the topic has passed its due date.

**Milestone** Topic milestone.

**Images** Previews of markups, 2D and 3D snapshots in the topic comments. Click on an image to see a preview of the image without having to load the topic page.

**Date** Topic creation date.

**Topic number** The number of the topic on Catenda. Click on this number to copy a link to the topic.

**Bottom icons** Amount of checked boxes. Amount of comments. Amount of linked documents.
