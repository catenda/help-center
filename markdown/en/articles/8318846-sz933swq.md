# Stage 1 - Step 1 - Project start

This article will help you get set up in your project. There are a couple of settings that most people configure when setting up their project. The steps that will be described here will help you make your project a good project to participate in!

## 1. **Reading information**

In a new project everyone can see everything. Allowing users to see the right information can make it more likely for them to interact and collaborate. It is also important to hide any sensitive information from prying eyes for security reasons but also to make people feel more comfortable among their known set of peers.

## 2. **Writing information**

In a new project everyone can also interact with everything. Letting users make changes in the project gives them the possibility to share information. It is a great thing to be open and share knowledge that someone else might be useful but there is such a thing as oversharing. It is therefore important to give people an arena to share information with an audience that is willing to listen.
