# Step 1 - Account setup

> Create your account and get invited to a project

An invitation is sent to you via email by Catenda with a link to the next steps of registration. Using this link you can create an account with your personal credentials.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/m6qttvze/01-intro.png)

After your registration is complete, you can access your projects on Catenda Hub from any web browser. [https://hub.catenda.com/signin](https://hub.catenda.com/signin) Further reading: [Sign in page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/7891486-sign-in-page), [Creating an account](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/8412895-creating-an-account), [Landing page](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/4670260-landing-page)

> **Note:** Find [step 2](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/10576075-step-2-interface) in [getting started](https://support.catenda.com/en/collections/2657208-getting-started) on the help center or in the help menu.
