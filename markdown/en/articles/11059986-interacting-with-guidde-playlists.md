## 2. **Export guide as document**

The videos in the playlist often get updated with changes on the platform. The latest information will therefore always be in the playlist. If you would like the step-by-step guide of a video as a document, please let us know which guide you would like the document for at [support@catenda.com](mailto:support@catenda.com) where we will be happy to assist. Document exports of guides can be requested in the following formats:

- MP4
- Gif
- Document PDF
- Slides PDF
- PPTX​

## 3. Sharing the guide

After opening the playlist you will be able to find the share button on the top right. This is what the share your playlist menu can look like:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/wm2jp1mz/01-sharing-the-guide.png)

Notice that your URL changes when you click on the different videos. When you share with the share button, you will share a link to your current video as part of the playlist.
