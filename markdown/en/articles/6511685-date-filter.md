# Date filter 📆

You can now filter by date on all your documents and issues here is how it works: 👇

In this example, we will see the date filters in the "**Issues**" section, the same steps are to be followed for the "**document**" section.

You are in the "topics" section

The filters are located on the left side panel:

1. In the "**Updated**" section click on "**Select dates**".

    <div class="intercom-container"><img height="1828" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/t3kkh5dt/inline-60a1bb50b7da.png" style="height: auto;" width="3360"/></div><div class="intercom-container"><img height="1828" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/t3kkh5dt/inline-84e66eb66553.png" style="height: auto;" width="3360"/></div>

    There are two possibilities:

    **A.** You can choose a tab in panel A: Today, yesterday, one week ago ...

    <div class="intercom-container"><img height="530" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/t3kkh5dt/inline-2a7e66b298c6.png" style="height: auto;" width="280"/></div>

OR

**B.** You can choose a specific date or period (start and end date)

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/t3kkh5dt/01-intro.png)

You can activate and deactivate your filter at any time, by checking/unchecking the "**updated**" box

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/t3kkh5dt/02-intro.png)
