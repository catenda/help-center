# Add to home screen

The full functionality of Catenda Hub is always available through the browser. Catenda supports being added to the home screen, dashboard or dock by installing it via a browser. This is otherwise known as a progressive web app (PWA). Click [here](https://support.catenda.com/en/articles/10774300-home-screen-system-browser-support) to see which browsers support PWA on your system. This article contains recommended suggestions for installing Catenda as a PWA on different systems.

## 1. **Benefits**

Installing Catenda via a browser instead of creating a shortcut gives the following benefits:

### 1.1 **Systems that are used by multiple people**

Installing Catenda Hub to your home screen lets you access Catenda in the core way applications are accessed on your system. Having it as part of a list of available applications can help people find what apps are available on this system.

### 1.2 **Systems with limited screen space**

Installing Catenda Hub as an app that is added to the home screen saves screen space as the browser bar towards the top will not be present. Especially on tablets it is nice to have the full Catenda functionality available while making the most of your screen space.

## 2. **iPhone & iPad - Recommended browser**

On Apple devices it is often only possible to add a page to your home screen from Safari. Navigate to the page you want to be opened when you open the application that will be added to the home screen of your device.

### 2.1 **Share button**

Tap the Share button that can look something like this.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/01-share-button.png)

The share button can appear to the right of the url bar or towards the bottom of the page.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/02-share-button.png)

### 2.2 **Add to home screen**

In the list of options that appear, scroll down until you see the "Add to Home Screen" action and choose it. The Add to Home Screen dialog box will now appear.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/03-add-to-home-screen.png)

Give the shortcut that will appear on your home screen a name. The link that will be opened is displayed so you can confirm it. You will also see the icon that the home screen shortcut will have.

### 2.3 **Add**

Click on "add" when you are done.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/04-add.png)

Safari will close and you will be taken to the place where the shortcut is installed on the home screen of your iPhone or iPad. The Catenda shortcut can look something like this on your home screen:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/05-add.png)

> **Note:** To remove the website shortcut, delete it just like you would any other app on your phone.

## 3. **Android - Recommended browser**

By installing Catenda as an app on your device you can add it to your home screen with the Chrome browser. In the Chrome browser, navigate to the page you want to be opened when you tap on the page will be added to the home screen of your device.

### 3.1 **Action menu**

Tap the hamburger menu (action menu with 3 dots towards the top right)

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/06-action-menu.png)

### 3.2 **Add to home screen**

In the action menu, tap Add to home screen.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/07-add-to-home-screen.png)

### 3.3 **Install or create shortcut**

Either Install or Create shortcut

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/08-install-or-create-shortcut.png)

**Create shortcut** Create a simple shortcut that will open the page you navigated to in the Chrome app.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/09-install-or-create-shortcut.png)

_Install_ (recommended) Installs the page to your home screen Installing Catenda will give you the best experience as you will not have Chrome bar towards the top. This allows you to use the whole screen of your device for browsing Catenda.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/10-install-or-create-shortcut.png)

This is what the browser shortcut and installed page can look like on your home screen:

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/catenda/help-center/main/images/sa3rvz1r/11-install-or-create-shortcut.png)

### 3.4 **Uninstalling**

To remove the Installed website or website shortcut, delete it just like you would any other app on your phone.
